Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for standard events, and 2-3 months for large-scale deployments (5,000+ attendees). However, we've successfully executed projects with as little as 2 weeks notice for returning clients. Contact us early to ensure availability.

What's your typical setup time for a large event?

For major events like DreamHack, we typically arrive 7-10 days before doors open. Our crew of 6-8 technicians works 12-hour shifts to deploy infrastructure. Smaller events (under 1,000 attendees) can often be set up in 2-3 days.

Do you provide the networking equipment or do we need to rent it?

We provide everything. Switches, access points, cabling, racks, UPS systems—all included in our service. We maintain our own inventory of enterprise-grade equipment from Cisco, Aruba, and Arista. You just need to provide power and an uplink from your ISP.

What happens if something goes wrong during the event?

Our team maintains an on-site NOC (Network Operations Center) with 24/7 monitoring throughout your event. We have redundant systems at every critical point, spare equipment ready to deploy, and our team is trained to resolve issues in minutes. We've maintained 99.9%+ uptime across all our events.

Can you work with our venue's existing infrastructure?

Absolutely. We've worked in convention centers, arenas, hotels, and outdoor venues worldwide. We'll do a site survey to understand existing infrastructure, coordinate with venue IT teams, and design a solution that integrates seamlessly. We bring everything needed to supplement venue capabilities.

Do you offer streaming/broadcast network support?

Yes! We provide dedicated broadcast networks with guaranteed bandwidth for Twitch/YouTube streams, NDI video transport, and production equipment. We've supported streams reaching 1M+ concurrent viewers. Our isolated broadcast VLANs ensure your stream never drops, even under heavy attendee traffic.
